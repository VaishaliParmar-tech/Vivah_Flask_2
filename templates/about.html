Our Story

About Vivah

Born from a love of Indian heritage and craftsmanship, Vivah is more than a saree brand — it's a celebration of womanhood.

Our Mission

Weaving Heritage Into Every Drape

Vivah was founded with a single purpose — to make the finest Indian sarees accessible to every woman who cherishes her culture. We work directly with master weavers from Varanasi, Kanchipuram, and Surat to bring you authentic, handcrafted pieces.

Each saree in our collection tells a story — of skilled hands, of generational knowledge, of India's unparalleled textile tradition. When you wear Vivah, you carry a piece of this heritage.
